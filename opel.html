2020 Opel Corsa GSI

The Opel Corsa GSI 1.4T showcases a striking and sporty exterior design that undoubtedly appeals to many enthusiasts. Notably, the front end is adorned with sporty elements, including the presence of black vents positioned between the bonnet and the front grille. Furthermore, the vehicle comes equipped with bi-xenon cornering headlights, a feature that not only enhances visibility but also significantly contributes to its overall aggressive appearance. Moving to the interior, the GSI offers a luxurious experience with its leather upholstery and the inclusion of the OPC Line Interior pack. This pack incorporates a sporty flat-bottom, perforated leather steering wheel, along with aluminum pedals. For infotainment, the car is equipped with a 7-inch touchscreen offering various connectivity options. A standout feature is the Recaro Performance seats, providing a supercar-like feel and enhancing the overall driving experience. Beneath the hood, the GSI boasts a turbocharged 1.4-liter engine, generating 110kW of power and 220Nm of torque, which is efficiently transmitted to the front wheels through a 6-speed manual gearbox. This powerful combination, in conjunction with the OPC suspension and braking system, ensures an exhilarating driving experience. The car's acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h is achieved in just 8.9 seconds, and it also impresses with its commendable fuel efficiency, averaging approximately 7 liters per 100 kilometers during testing.

Price: R289 500
